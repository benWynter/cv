I’m the General Manager at Anytime Fitness in Tulse Hill and a talented linguist being bilingual in both English and French with a strong command of Spanish also. Prior to working in the fitness industry, I worked in IT recruitment where I had the opportunity to learn about the world of IT which piqued my interest; to the point that I began learning the basics of C language in my free time in conjunction with the CS50x course from Harvard University. I am currently learning web development (HTML, CSS & JavaScript, the latter of which I have a particular interest in) as well as C# and Python with the goal of making a career change into web/software development.
